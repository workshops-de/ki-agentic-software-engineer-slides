# Why Agentic Workflows?

Without automation, routine development tasks consume time. With agentic workflows, AI handles them.

- **Manual reviews** take hours; automated reviews run in minutes
- **Repetitive fixes** follow the same patterns — agents execute them
- **Quality gates** get bypassed under pressure; workflows enforce them
- **GitHub Actions** provide the event-driven backbone agents need
