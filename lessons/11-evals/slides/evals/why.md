# Why Evals?

Without measuring, you can't tell if your prompts and instructions actually work.

- **Instructions vary** — small changes can break or improve generated code
- **Models behave differently** — what works for one may fail for another
- **Guessing wastes time** — evals give you data instead of opinions
- **Reports and history** — compare runs and improve systematically
