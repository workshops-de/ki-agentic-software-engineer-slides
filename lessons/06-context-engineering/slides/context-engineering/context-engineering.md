# Why this topic matters

Context is key to solving tasks; without understanding it, developers get wrong answers and bad-quality output.

- Developers need to understand that context is key to solving tasks
- Otherwise they stumble across wrong answers and bad-quality output
- Context can be polluted or become worse over time
- That's why it is a must to understand the parts of the context

# Limits and quality

- Science is still evolving; findings may shift
- When context reaches **~60%** of its limit, quality tends to decrease
- **200K or 2M tokens** is not that much for complex business problems

<br>
<br>

<Callout type="tip">
Keep context focused and well-structured to get the best results within limits.
</Callout>
